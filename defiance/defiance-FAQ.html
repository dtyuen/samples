GENERAL QUESTIONS

What is Defiance?

Defiance™ is a collaboration between Trion Worlds and Syfy that aims to be the next revolutionary step in transmedia entertainment. Both a massive online shooter for console and PC and a television show, Defiance unfolds in a single immersive universe that will evolve on a week-by-week basis. Simply put: this project is unlike anything that has ever been attempted before. The video game is a 3rd-person futuristic online open-world shooter where thousands of players compete for Votan technology that falls to the Earth in events called “Arkfalls”. You can choose to play as a human or alien Ark Hunter, diving into vast and varied gameplay and customization options that will keep you engaged for years to come.

Who is developing the game?

Defiance is being developed internally by a very talented team at Trion Worlds.

What platforms is it available on?

Defiance is available for PC, PlayStation®3 and Xbox 360®.

Is there a Collector’s Edition?

There is a limited-run Collector’s Edition with bonus items including a Hellbug statue, art book, soundtrack, in-game items, and more. There will also be a very limited Ultimate Edition run, complete with Defiance messenger bag. Check out the #buy now tab# for more details.

Can I buy a digital version of the game?

Yes, we will be selling digital versions of the basic game for PC, PS3, and Xbox 360. There will also be a Digital Deluxe version of the game containing tons of digital content at an incredible value as well as a digital upgrade option for those customers who bought the standard edition and decide to upgrade to the Digital Deluxe edition. PC and PS3 digital versions will be available at launch. As per Xbox Live’s guidelines a digital verson of the Xbox 360 game will not be available until 3 months after launch. Check out the Digital section of the #buy now tab# for more detail.

Will there be downloadable content after the game launches?

Yes, both free and paid content updates are planned on a regular basis after launch.

How does the game interact with the show?

Defiance is a brand new futuristic sci fi universe created by Syfy and Trion Worlds in partnership. As a joint universe there are many similarities such as characters, races, events etc. that they each share. But the game and the show also have unique identities, story archs and plotlines and can be enjoyed as seperate entertainment entities. However, the experience of Defiance is enhanced when both game and show are played/watched together because there are regular show and game integrations that can take the form of an in-game event, character crossover, themed celebration etc. Details of game/show cross overs will be announced on Defiance.com.

What is the plot of Defiance?

Set in the near future, Defiance introduces a completely transformed planet Earth inhabited by the human and alien survivors of a global catastrophe. Forced to live and work together, this disparate group struggles to build a new society among the devastation. In the game, you take on the role of an Ark Hunter, a treasure/bounty hunter recently arrived in the San Francisco Bay Area in search of valuable Votan technology. Almost unrecognizable after three decades of war and terraforming, the Bay has become a remote and wild place, home to both humans and Votans bent on claiming fame and fortune in the new frontier. In the show, the mysterious Nolan, drifting through a terraformed American landscape, settles in a border town where Votans and humans defend a fragile peace.

How do I get my Ark Hunter Rewards?

Players who are on console will be able to open their in-game menu, and go into settings to select the Link Account option. Once choosing this option, you will be provided with a code, which can be applied to your Trion Worlds account. Logging into your Trion Worlds account, and going to Settings, you will see the option to Link an Account. Here is where your console code will be applied to link your account. Then any Pre-order or Arkfall unlocks that you have earned will be redeemable on your console characters.

Players who are accessing Defiance through a different retailer such as Steam, will be able to launch Defiance from their provider’s game library, to see the Defiance patcher. As long as you are logging into the Defiance Patcher using your Trion Worlds account details, you should see any Pre-order items or Arkfall unlocks that were applied to the account.

GAMEPLAY QUESTIONS

Will I be able to customize my character?

Yes, you will be able to create your own class, modify and upgrade your weapons, and customize the look of your characters, vehicles, and more. In addition, you will have a range of perks and power choices to make as you progress, so no one character will be the same.

What kind of multiplayer options will there be?

Defiance offers fast and furious competitive mulitplayer in all-out Team Deathmatch and Capture and Hold multiplayer maps, as well as the Shadow War: a massive open-world (and over-world!) team combat mode featuring incredible rewards. More information can be found in the #Game section#.

Will there be co-op?

Defiance offers co-op adventures both in the form of instances and dynamic Arkfall events.

Will I be able to play on my own?

Although Defiance supports a massive number of players in co-op and competitive multiplayer games, you don’t have to join a team to jump in and have fun. You can play as a lone wolf across the new frontier, but it definitely benefits you to join others in more challenging fights.

What is EGO and how does it effect my game?

The Environmental Guardian Online, or EGO, is a symbiotic neuro-muscular bionetic implant designed by Votanis Collective scientists. It was intended to boost the battle prowess of Votan soldiers. EGO implants provide combat support and supply intelligence and diagnostics through a complex local area network known as the Ark-Net. Ark hunters in the Bay Area Expedition have all been implanted. You will track your progress and make changes to your character via the EGO interface. EGO also provides you with information about enemies, activities, and events you encounter and briefs you on side-mission locations you can explore across the world.

What are Arkfalls?

The Arks were enormous alien ships that transported the Votans to Earth. During the struggles after the Votans’ arrival, the Arks were destroyed in near-Earth orbit, where their debris created what is known as the Arkbelt. From time to time, a portion of this debris enters the atmosphere and plummets to Earth, creating an Arkfall. The alien debris from the Arks frequently includes valuable resources and technology, making it a prime target for Ark Hunters, hellbugs, and other deadly scavengers. Arkfalls appear randomly either as single meteorites or as a cluster and can lead to very large, hectic battles depending on the number of players and/or boss encounters that come with them!

What are Ark Hunter Rewards and why should I collect them?

Ark Hunter Rewards is an Ark Hunter training program outside of the game where players can sign up and enter unique 6 digit codes to unlock special in-game items & rewards. These ark fall codes are hidden in various Defiance promotional assets, trailers, websites etc. Players need to sign up to become an ark hunter on Defiance.com/arkhunter. The more codes you collect and enter the more rewards you can unlock. #Collect and enter more codes#

How do I level up in Defiance?

Defiance doesn’t have levels in the typical sense of many MMOs. Your EGO Rating increases as you gain Experience, complete Pursuits, and increase your Skills. As your rating increases, you will find a greater variety of better gear in the world, and you will unlock new content and character benefits. In addition to EGO Rating benefits, gaining Experience gives you EGO Units to upgrade Perks and Abilities, while increasing your Weapon or Vehicle Skills improves your effectiveness with all weapons or vehicles of that type.

Will there be vendors in-game?

Typically players can locate Vendors scattered around Defiance. These Vendors appear to be large metal containers, which allow a player to walk up and interact with them, and can be found typically in Friendly strongholds. Most vendors can be seen when a player opens their world map. Placing your mouse cursor over any specific Vendor seen on the Map should provide information on what type of items the Vendor sells, such as Mods, Weapons or Lock Boxes. Most Vendors in-game will allow a player to pay for their goods with Scrip. However, some vendors require specific currency to be earned by completing “Contracts” in order to purchase stronger and more are items. Completing contracts for different Factions, such as Von Bach Industries or Echelon Mercenaries allows you to earn currency that can be used at Vendors with items for their factions. Players can also find Vendors providing different special items which go on sale at different moments. Different items are offered to players randomly from these Vendors.

How can I travel quickly while in-game?

Fast Travel:
Fast travel is a feature that allows players to move quickly across large sections of the map. In order to fast travel, you will need to open your map and find the blue ‘Fast Travel’ icon. After double clicking on the icon you will be asked if you are sure you would like to fast travel, confirming this notification will send you to the location you have selected. Fast Travel does not cost scrip.

Extraction:
When a player is immobilized or dies is combat, you will be given the option to extract, which will take you back to your last checkpoint. You can also select your character’s icon in the map, the select Extract option in the bottom left hand corner of screen. If you log out and then back into the game, you will pick up at your last extraction point.

Stuck? Can’t use Fast Travel?:
If you find yourself getting stuck while playing Defiance, you can fix this by logging out or quiting the game. When you return a few minutes later you will be at the nearest extraction point.

I Participated in the Alpha/Beta, will I get to keep my character after launch?

Players who participated in the early Alpha/Beta events will not be able to keep characters created in these early environments.

What races can I choose from when playing Defiance?

Upon starting your character creation, players will have the ability to choose from Male/Female Irathient and Human races.

ACCOUNT QUESTIONS

Do I need to sign up for a Trion account to play the game?

If you choose to play the game on PC or take part in any of the beta events or reward programs, you will need to create a Trion account. However, if you just want to play the game on your console and do not want to take part in any external events or activities, you just need to have an Xbox 360 or PSN account.

Can PC and console players connect and play with each other?

No, cross-platform play will not be available.

How many characters can I create?

Initially, you can create up to four characters.

How does my character progress?

Player characters advance by improving their proficiency with many different weapon types, by upgrading their EGO Rating, which determines overall effectiveness, and by spending EGO Units (earned by improving EGO Rating) to earn and improve EGO Powers and EGO Perks. Players can also attach mods to their weapons to further improve and customize their gameplay experience.

Is there split-screen multiplayer for consoles?

No.

Will there be crafting?

Salvage Matrix is a basic crafting-type system that allows Ark Hunters to break down items into resources that can be used to upgrade gear. Use of the Salvage Matrix is a real-time process: players can start it via EGO, then leave the Salvage Matrix working while they do other things.

Will there be traditional MMO content? Small-group dungeons and large-group raids?

Defiance features small-group instances (similar to dungeons), and large-group dynamic events.

Will I need multiple accounts if I play on multiple platforms?

A player will have to link their one Trion Worlds account to multiple platforms. For example, if you have unlocked Arkfall codes on your Trion account, and linked an Xbox Live and PSN account to your single Trion worlds account, you will be able to see these unlocks for the characters you have created on your Xbox and PSN accounts.

PAYMENT QUESTIONS

Will there be a subscription?

There is no subscription required to play Defiance on PC or PS3 or Xbox 360. However if you buy the game on Xbox 360 new, you will need to have an Xbox Live Gold membership in order to play.

What methods of payment will I be able to use?

For PC, Trion Worlds accepts Visa, Master Card, Discover, American Express, and PayPal. Game Time Cards will also be available at major retailers. Payment methods through the PlayStation store and Xbox Live may differ, and you should refer to those stores for payment details.

What types of things will be sold in the in-game store?

Our store will offer a wide range of item categories including cosmetic outfits, additional vehicle looks and colors, headgear, boosts, and other items to enhance your character customization.
